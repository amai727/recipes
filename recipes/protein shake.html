Protein Shake

Description

Protein is one of the most essential nutrients to promote growth in your body. Many people's protein intake in their diets usually fall below the required amount according to the World Health Organization (WHO). This Protein Shake recipe consists of 45g of protein and 950 calories and will definitely help you achieve your daily protein goal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of oats
  • 1 scoop of whey protein
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil (optional for extra calories)
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 2 bananas

Steps

  1. Blend the oats in the blender
  2. Put the whey protein in
  3. Cut the banana into pieces and put them in
  4. Pour the milk first and put the peanut butter and olive oil in
  5. Pour the drink into your bottle
